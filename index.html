A Little About Shelby

I am a long-time Floridian who hasn’t grown tired of the state’s tourist treasures — from my favorite hangout, Walt Disney World, to Florida’s picturesque, white sandy beaches. I graduated from the University of Central Florida with a bachelor’s degree in interdiscplinary studies, focusing on psychology and writing, which led to a career in communication and a passion for creative writing.

In my current role as Employee Communications Coordinator at Valencia College, I collaborate with cross-functional teams to brainstorm and conceptualized original ideas for development of various types of employee communication - both digital and print. Because I am the coordinator, I am involved in all stages of the work - from brainstorming and concepting to content development and design to implementation and delivery.

Another facet of my work includes project management, communication planning and strategic work. Specifically, for project management, I am directly responsible for projects that require graphic design, video, tech support and website edits. I am also involved with website development, creating communication plans and acting as a liaison for photography projects.

Dring my tenure at Valencia College, I led two major system transitions — one for editorial management (DivvyHQ) and the other for our email marketing management system (PoliteMail), both of which required systems thinking, organization, attention to detail and adaptation. I was also actively involved in our project management system transition (LiquidPlanner), too, contributing to the strategic design of workflows and system development.

In my free time, when I'm not taking in Florida’s gems, I'm spending time with family, spending too much money on Pokemon Go, standing in line for another rice bowl at Sus-Hi Eatstation, writing superpower-inspired science fiction, or studying The Standard Book of Spells, Grade 1. (I'm still waiting for that letter from Hogwarts … pesky owls.).
